College Admissions Q&A

Ask a question or provide an answer about college admissions.

Q: When is the application deadline for Fall 2025 admissions?
A: The deadline is January 15, 2025.
Q: What documents are required for the application?
A: You need your transcripts, test scores, recommendation letters, and a personal essay.
Q: Is financial aid available for international students?
A: Yes, there are scholarships and grants specifically for international applicants.
